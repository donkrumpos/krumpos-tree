# John Schmidt

**Lifespan:** c. 1822 – ?

## Summary
Father of Charles Frederick Schmidt (1864–1920). Born 1822 in Germany. Married Elizabeth Rabe. Five children. Lived in Cleveland, Le Sueur County, Minnesota (1880) and Columbus, Franklin County, Ohio (1910).

## Relationships
- **Wife:** Elizabeth Rabe (Dec 12, 1846–1918, born Germany/Prussia) — 24-year age gap
- **Children:**
  - Elizabeth Schmidt (c. 1856–1918, born Illinois; not in 1880 household, likely married)
  - Mary Schmidt (Dec 15, 1859–Mar 28, 1909, born Illinois)
  - Charles Frederick Schmidt (Dec 13, 1864–May 16, 1920, born Joliet, IL)
  - Amelia Ann Schmidt (c. 1866–?, born Minnesota; age 14 in 1880 census)
  - Emilie Schmidt (1882–1966)

## Research Notes
- Born 1822 in **Mecklenburg** (now Mecklenburg-Vorpommern, northeast Germany) — confirmed immigrant
- Trail: Germany → Illinois (by 1856, eldest daughter Elizabeth born) → Joliet, Will Co, IL (1864) → Cleveland, Le Sueur Co, MN (by 1866, Amelia born) → Columbus, Franklin Co, OH (1910)
- 1880 census household: John (58), Elizabeth (56), Mary (20), Charles (16), Amelia (14). Daughter Elizabeth not present — already married.
- Living in Columbus, Ohio in 1910 — age 88. Not in son Charles's Minnesota household. May have relocated to be near one of his daughters.
- FamilySearch lists both "Emilie Schmidt (1882–1966)" and "Amelia Ann Schmidt (1882–Deceased)" — but 1880 census clearly shows Amelia age 14 (born c. 1866). FamilySearch's 1882 birth year for Amelia Ann is wrong. Emilie is a separate, younger child born 1882 — a 16-year gap after Charles, when Elizabeth was 36.
