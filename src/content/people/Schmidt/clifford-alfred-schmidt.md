# Clifford Alfred Schmidt

**Lifespan:** November 12, 1918 – September 6, 1991

## Summary
Born November 12, 1918 in Cleveland, Le Sueur County, Minnesota. Son of Frederick John L Schmidt and Mary Jane Kendall. Married Helen M. Martin in 1944 in Sturgeon Bay, Door County, Wisconsin. Lived in Preble, Brown County, WI (1950). Died September 6, 1991 at Crooked Lake, Wisconsin. Buried at Nicolet Memorial Gardens, Green Bay.
