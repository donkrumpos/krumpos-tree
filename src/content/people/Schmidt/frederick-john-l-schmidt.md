# Frederick John L Schmidt

**Lifespan:** January 3, 1888 – January 31, 1939

## Summary
Born January 3, 1888 in Cordova Township, Le Sueur County, Minnesota. Son of Charles Frederick Schmidt (age 23 at Frederick's birth) and Maria Katharina Wilhelmina Vollmer (age 24). Married Mary Jane Kendall on March 19, 1912 in Le Center, Le Sueur County, Minnesota. Eight children: seven sons and one daughter. Died January 31, 1939 in Cordova Township, Le Sueur County, Minnesota, age 51. Buried in Elysian, Le Sueur County, Minnesota.
