# Mary Jane Kendall

**Lifespan:** December 11, 1893 – 1971

## Summary
Born December 1893 in Lexington Township, Le Sueur County, Minnesota. Daughter of John H. Kendall and Margaret (Schindler) Kendall. Married Frederick John L Schmidt on March 19, 1912 in Le Center, Le Sueur County, Minnesota. Eight children (7 sons, 1 daughter). Lived in Cordova Township, Le Sueur County, Minnesota for about 10 years. Died 1971. Buried in Cleveland, Le Sueur County, Minnesota.
