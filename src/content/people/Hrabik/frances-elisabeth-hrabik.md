# Frances Elisabeth Hrabik

**Lifespan:** c. October 1865 – October 30, 1929

## Summary
Born c. October 1865 in Czechoslovakia. Died October 30, 1929 in Oconto, Oconto County, Wisconsin. Married Rudolph Kosnar on August 22, 1885 in Kewaunee, Wisconsin (she was 18–19, he was 25). Mother of Frances Josephine Kosnar. Buried at Evergreen Cemetery, Oconto, Oconto County, Wisconsin.
