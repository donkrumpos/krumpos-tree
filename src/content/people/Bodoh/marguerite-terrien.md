# Marguerite Terrien

**Lifespan:** 1813-12-28 — 1903-01-18

## Summary
Born in St Luc, St Jean, Québec, Canada to Louis Terrien (b. 1794, L'Acadie) and Marguerite Tetreau (b. 1795, Chambly). Married Henry Lajeunesse on February 5, 1833 in L'Acadie, Quebec. Mother of Peter Young/Lajeunesse (1847–1918).

Died January 18, 1903 in Fall River, Bristol County, Massachusetts at age 89. Fall River was a major French-Canadian mill town — the family diaspora split between Wisconsin (Peter) and New England textile centers (Marguerite, and likely other children).
