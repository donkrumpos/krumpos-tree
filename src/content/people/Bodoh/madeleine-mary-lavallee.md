# Madeleine Mary Lavallee

**Lifespan:** 1816-02-28 — 1899-04-01

## Summary
Born in La Prairie, Quebec to Alexis Lavallee (1793, La Prairie — d. 1868, Rouses Point, Clinton County, NY) and Marie Amable Langevin (1792, Varennes). Married Joseph Guyette. Died April 1, 1899 in Fond du Lac County — same day her daughter Julie Guyette died in Maple Creek.
