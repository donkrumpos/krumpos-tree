# Josephine LeBlanc

**Lifespan:** 1822-10-10 — 1901-12-17

## Summary
Born in Montreal to Jacques LeBlanc (1797, Chambly) and Mary Louise Masson (~1795). Married Moises Balthazar. Died December 17, 1901 in Maple Creek — same community where Julie Guyette (d. 1899) and Elnora Surprise (d. 1904) died.

LeBlanc immigrant ancestor: Jacques LeBlanc (1644, France) & Marie Suzanne Rousselin (1644, France) — died 2 days apart in April 1710.
