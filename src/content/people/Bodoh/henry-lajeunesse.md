# Henry Lajeunesse

**Lifespan:** 1803-08-13 — ?

## Summary
Born in Québec, Canada to Antoine Lajeunesse (1776–1851) and Marie Genevieve Demers (1781–1842). Married Marguerite Terrien on February 5, 1833 in L'Acadie, Quebec. Father of Peter Young/Lajeunesse (1847–1918). The family name **Lajeunesse** was Anglicized to **Young** by Peter's generation in Wisconsin.

## Relationships
