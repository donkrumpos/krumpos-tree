# Joseph Guyette

**Lifespan:** 1811-10-19 — 1902-01-11

## Summary
Born in Canada to Joseph Goyette (1788–1876, age 87) and Marguerite Bouthillier (1788). Married Madeleine Mary Lavallee on February 22, 1830 in Iberville, Quebec. 11 children. Emigrated to Wisconsin. Died January 11, 1902 in Fond du Lac County at age 90.
