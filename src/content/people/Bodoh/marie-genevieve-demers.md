# Marie Genevieve Demers

**Lifespan:** 1781-01-28 — 1842-06-30

## Summary
Born in Iberville, Quebec. Married Antoine Lajeunesse on January 12, 1801 in Chambly, Quebec. Mother of Henry Lajeunesse (b. 1803). Died June 30, 1842 in Richelieu, Quebec at age 61. Never left Quebec.
