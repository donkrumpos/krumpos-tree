# Noel Morin (Immigrant)

**Lifespan:** 1616 — 1679-02-10

## Summary
**IMMIGRANT.** Born in Brittany, France to Claude Morin (1591, St Étienne, Loire, France) and Jeanne Moreau (1595). Married Helene Desportes — one of the first European children born in New France — on January 9, 1640 in Québec City. Helene's first husband was Guillaume Augustin Hebert (connected to the Hébert founding family).
