# Antoine Lajeunesse

**Lifespan:** 1776-08-05 — 1851-03-04

## Summary
Born in St Antoine de Padoue, Longueuil, Chambly, Quebec. Married Marie Genevieve Demers on January 12, 1801 in Chambly, Quebec. Father of Henry Lajeunesse (b. 1803). Died March 4, 1851 in Iberville, Quebec at age 74. Never left Quebec.
