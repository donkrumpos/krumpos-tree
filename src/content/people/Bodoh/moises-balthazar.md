# Moises Balthazar

**Lifespan:** 1820-09-30 — 1890-10-05

## Summary
Born in Québec City to Martin Balthazar (~1790) and Marie Sophie Hebert (1799–1892, age 93). Married Josephine LeBlanc (1822–1901, Montreal). Father of Julia Marie Balthazar (1844–1912). Died October 5, 1890 in Bear Creek. Buried at St Mary Cemetery, Bear Creek.

Grandfather Martin Balthazar (1730, Lorraine, France) was the immigrant ancestor.
