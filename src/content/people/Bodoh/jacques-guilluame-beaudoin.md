# Jacques Guilluame Beaudoin

**Lifespan:** 1812 — December 1874

## Summary
Born in Plattsburgh, Clinton County, New York to Louis Marie Beaudoin (1755, Quebec City) and Marie Louise Jolivette (~1755). Married Louise Lemonde on September 30, 1828 in Québec City. Father of Jacques Guilluame Bodoh (1836–1918). Died December 1874 in Duck Creek, Brown County, Wisconsin.

The family name **Beaudoin** became **Bodoh** in his son's generation.
