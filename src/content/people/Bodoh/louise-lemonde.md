# Louise Lemonde

**Lifespan:** 1813 — 1895-11-28

## Summary
Born in Beloeil, Verchères, Quebec to Louis Lemonde (~1786) and Marie Anne Christine Tanguay (~1786). Married Jacques Guilluame Beaudoin. Died November 28, 1895 in Bear Creek, Outagamie County. Buried at St Mary Cemetery, Bear Creek — same cemetery as Peter Young and Elnora Surprise.
