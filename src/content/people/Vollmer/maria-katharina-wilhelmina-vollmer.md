# Maria Katharina Wilhelmina Vollmer

**Lifespan:** February 28, 1863 – September 10, 1905

## Summary
Born February 28, 1863 in Freeport, Stephenson County, Illinois. Daughter of Christian Vollmer (age 48 at her birth) and Anna Maria Kurtz (age 25). Married Charles Frederick Schmidt on December 13, 1883 in St. Peter, Nicollet County, Minnesota. At least 2 sons and 8 daughters. Lived in Cleveland, Le Sueur County, MN (1900). Died September 10, 1905, age 42. Buried at German Lake Lutheran Cemetery, Elysian, Le Sueur County, Minnesota.

## Research Notes
- Born in Illinois like husband Charles — both second-generation German-American
- Another large parental age gap: Christian Vollmer was 48, Anna Maria Kurtz was 25 (23-year gap) — same pattern as John Schmidt (42) and Elizabeth Rabe (18)
- Died at 42 — confirms why she was absent from Charles's 1910 census household. Charles was a widower for 15 years.
- Buried at German Lake **Lutheran** Cemetery — indicates this family was Lutheran, unlike the Catholic Krumpos/Stefl/Bohemian lines
- Married on Charles's 19th birthday (both Dec 13)
- 1900 census lists her as "Minnie" — nickname for Wilhelmina
- 1900 census: her parents listed as born in Germany. But FamilySearch says she was born in Freeport, Illinois — so she's second-generation, parents were the immigrants.

- "Tilly" from 1910 census was actually **Lilly E.** (b. Feb 1893) — T/L handwriting confusion
