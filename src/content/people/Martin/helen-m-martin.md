# Helen M. Martin
**Lifespan:** November 13, 1924 – June 7, 1974

## Summary
Born November 13, 1924 in Sturgeon Bay, Door County, Wisconsin, daughter of **Gerald Martin Sr.** (d. May 1931) and **Hazel P. Mielke** (1902–1987). Married **Clifford Alfred Schmidt** in 1944 in Sturgeon Bay. Lived in Preble, Brown County, WI (1950). Died June 7, 1974 in Green Bay, Brown County, Wisconsin, age 50. Buried at Nicolet Memorial Gardens, Green Bay.
