# Mildred Amelia Guernsey Martin

**Lifespan:** March 21, 1880 – December 15, 1971

## Summary
Born March 21, 1880 in Ionia, Michigan. Maiden name Guernsey. Married Lile/Life Martin. Lived on Peter Street, Sturgeon Bay, Door County, Wisconsin. At least three sons: Gerald, John, Henry. Died December 15, 1971 in Sturgeon Bay, age 91. Buried in Sturgeon Bay.
