# Lester E. Martin Sr.

**Lifespan:** c. 1926 – between 1987 and 2011 (exact dates unknown)

## Summary
Born around 1926 in Sturgeon Bay, Wisconsin — middle child of Gerald Martin Sr. and Hazel Mielke. Age 5 when his father Gerald died of a two-month illness in June 1931. Raised by Hazel and (after her 1932 remarriage) stepfather Clarence Lau. Married Lila Rickel. Settled in Townsend, Oconto County, Wisconsin, where he raised at least eight children. Living in Townsend as of 1987 (per Hazel's obituary). Deceased before April 2011 (preceded son Lester F. Jr. in death).

## Relationship to Don
**Great-uncle** — brother of Don's grandmother Helen M. Martin.
