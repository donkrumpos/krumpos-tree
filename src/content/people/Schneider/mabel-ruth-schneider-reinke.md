# Mabel Ruth Schneider Reinke

**Lifespan:** December 23, 1908 – April 1, 1992

## Summary
Born in Kenosha, Wisconsin. Died at age 83 in Green Bay. Sister of Matilda Henrietta (Schneider) Krumpos (1897–1973) and Paul Schneider. Married Harold Reinke in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, 1947. No children. Member of Grace Lutheran Church, Green Bay. Moved from Cedar Rapids to Green Bay in 1957. Buried at Mount Calvary Cemetery, De Pere.
