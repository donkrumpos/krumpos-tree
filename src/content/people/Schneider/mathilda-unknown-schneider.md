# Mathilda (Unknown) Schneider

**Lifespan:** April 1870 – July 1912

## Summary
Mother of Matilda Henrietta (Schneider) Krumpos (1897–1973). Born April 1870 in Germany. Died c. 1912 in Kenosha, Wisconsin (age ~42). Buried at Green Ridge Cemetery, Kenosha, Kenosha County, Wisconsin. Maiden name unknown.

## Relationships
- **Husband:** Von Wilhem Schneider (b. c. 1872, Germany)
- **Children:**
  - Matilda Henrietta Schneider (Jan 25, 1897–1973) — 2nd wife of Lawrence Antone Krumpos
  - Mabel Ruth Schneider (Dec 23, 1908–Apr 1, 1992) — married Harold Reinke
  - Paul Schneider — married Ida
