# Lawrence Antone Krumpos

**Lifespan:** May 2, 1889 – July 28, 1986

## Summary
Patriarch of the Krumpos family. Born May 2, 1889 in Oconto, Oconto County, Wisconsin. Self-employed machinist; retired 1957. Last known residence: 811 Oak St., De Pere (per Mathilda's 1973 obituary). Died July 28, 1986 at a local nursing home in De Pere, Brown County, Wisconsin, age 97. Two marriages: Frances Josephine (Kosnar), married June 28, 1911 in Menominee, Michigan; then Matilda Henrietta (Schneider), married April 9, 1922 in Kewaunee, Wisconsin. Buried at Mount Calvary Cemetery, De Pere — Section CA, Lot 24-C.

## Research Notes
- Born in Oconto, Wisconsin
- Half-siblings all born in Oconto area (Little River, Oconto) — children of Joseph's unknown first wife
- Johanna Katherine was his only full sibling (same parents)
- Parents married Dec 6, 1884 in Little River, Oconto, WI
- ~~TIMELINE CONFLICT~~ **RESOLVED:** Marriage to Matilda was April 9, 1922 in Kewaunee, WI — confirmed by his obituary. FamilySearch's "1919/age 21" derives from an earlier erroneous year. Frances died 1921, Lawrence remarried April 9, 1922. Sequence confirmed.
- Buried at Mount Calvary Cemetery, De Pere, Brown County, Wisconsin — Section CA, Lot 24-C, paired with Matilda in adjacent Lot 24-D
- Occupation: self-employed machinist; retired 1957
- Final known residence: 811 Oak St., De Pere (confirmed by Mathilda's 1973 obit); died at a local nursing home in 1986
- Funeral arrangements: Ryan Funeral Home, 305 N. Tenth St., De Pere; Rev. Charles Brooks officiating; burial at Mount Calvary Cemetery
- Descendants at time of death (1986): 31 grandchildren, 22 great-grandchildren, 7 great-great-grandchildren
- **Sibling-count discrepancy — research target:** Obituary states he was preceded in death by "four brothers and four sisters" (8 siblings total). Current records show 7 — three half-brothers (Joseph Samuel, James Ernest, Matthew Jacob), three half-sisters (Mary, Anna, Josephine), and one full sister (Johanna Katherine). One brother appears to be missing from the record. Likely a half-brother from Joseph Frank's first marriage.
- Granddaughter "Katherine Ann" preceded him in death (per obituary): this is Catherine Ann Krumpos (1952–1965), Donald Howard's daughter. Spelling variant in the obit ("Katherine" → "Catherine"). See [catherine-krumpos.md].
- Krumpos family monument at Mount Calvary is a "fallen sons" memorial. Central inscription: *"In memory of our sons who gave their lives in defense of their country / Holy Mary have mercy on us."* Names two KIA sons:
  - **Pvt. Lester F.** (1918–1944) — killed Nov 30, 1944 at Metz, France (son of 1st wife Frances)
  - **Corp. Jerome M.** (1928–1950) — killed Aug 30, 1950 at Seoul, Korea (son of Matilda)
  Side plaques: 1st Wife Frances (1888–1921), Father Lawrence A. (1889–1986), Mother Mathilda H. (1897–1973), Son Jerome M. (1928–1950), and **Mabel R. Reinke (1908–1992)** — Mathilda's sister, who chose burial with the Krumpos family rather than with the Reinke or Schneider lines.
- **Lester F. Krumpos (1918–1944)** appears in the children list and on the family monument but has no person file yet. Son of Frances Kosnar. KIA at Metz, France during WWII. Worth creating.
