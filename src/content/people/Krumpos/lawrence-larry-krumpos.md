# Lawrence F. Krumpos Jr. (Larry)

**Lifespan:** June 14, 1930 – June 6, 2013

## Summary
Born June 14, 1930 in Green Bay, Brown County, Wisconsin. Died June 6, 2013 in DePere, Brown County, Wisconsin. Owned Larry’s De Pere Ambulance; coached 37 yrs; helped start Kelly Danen Park.

## Relationships
- **Parents:** Lawrence Antone Krumpos (1889–1986) & Matilda Henrietta Schneider (1897–1973)
- **Spouse:** Shirley Baeten (m. 1952)
- **Children:** Dr. Gerald Lawrence Krumpos, Lori Ann Krumpos, Lynn Theresa Krumpos, Lisa Mary Krumpos, Jeffrey Krumpos
