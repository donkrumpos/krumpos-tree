# Joseph Frank Krumpos
## Summary
Full name **Joseph Frank Krumpos**. Born October 16, 1845 in Trhanov, Bohemia (near Domazlice). Immigrated 1867. Died February 19, 1920 in Oconto, Oconto Co, Wisconsin. Married twice. Six children from first (unknown) wife, then two children with second wife Mary Katherine (Stefl), married December 6, 1884 in Oconto County, Wisconsin.

## Relationships
- **1st wife:** Josephine (c. 1852, Bohemia — both parents Bohemian per 1880 census). Died before Dec 1884 (when Joseph married 2nd wife). Mother of:
  - Mary Krumpos (1871) — m. Lafortune
  - Anna Krumpos (1873) — m. Lafortune (same family as Mary's husband)
  - Joseph Samuel Krumpos (1876, Oconto, WI) — m. Anna H. Wasurick, Oct 8, 1902, Oconto, WI. Children: Josephine Phyllis (m. Bennett), Earl Joseph, Carroll Edward, Roland Joseph.
  - Josephine Krumpos (1878) — m. Connor
  - James Ernest Krumpos (1880)
  - Vaclav Krumpos (c. Jan 1880, WI) — 5 months old in 1880 census. Czech name (= Wenceslaus). Likely died young or was later known as James Ernest.
  - James Ernest Krumpos (1880) — may be Vaclav anglicized, or a separate child born after Vaclav's death
  - Matthew Jacob Krumpos (1882)
- **2nd wife:** Mary Katherine Stefl (Apr 16, 1845–Mar 25, 1920) — m. Dec 6, 1884, Oconto County, Wisconsin
- **Children with 2nd wife:**
  - Johanna Katherine Krumpos (c. Jul 1886–1936) — m. Ley
  - Lawrence Antone Krumpos (1889–1986) — **direct line**

## Research Notes
- Born in **Trhanov, Bohemia** (near Domazlice, western Bohemia). WikiTree listed as "Trogan" — anglicized rendering of Trhanov. Village is ~5 km from Domazlice, where wife Mary Katherine Stefl was from. Same Chodsko region. They likely knew each other in the old country.
- Trhanov parish records are digitized at **portafontium.cz** — search for baptism c. 1845-1847 under Krumpos/Krumpus to find parents.
- Immigrated 1867
- Died in Oconto, just one month before second wife Mary Katherine
- Little Chute Genealogy lists birth as Oct 16, 1845; WikiTree previously had May 1847 — two-year discrepancy
- Mary Katherine's death: Little Chute says Mar 25, WikiTree said Mar 26 — one-day discrepancy
- Mary Katherine's birthplace per Little Chute: Domazlice, Okres Domazlice, Plzensky (Plzen), Czech Republic
- **First wife identified: Josephine** — per 1880 Oconto County census. Age 28, born Bohemia, both parents Bohemian. Maiden name unknown. Died between 1880 and Dec 1884 (when Joseph married Mary Katherine Stefl). Age 28 in 1880 → born c. 1852.
- **Vaclav** — baby in 1880 census, 5 months old. Czech name. Not in later records — either died young or was anglicized to James Ernest (both born c. 1880).
- **Parents both Bohemian** — 1880 census confirms Joseph's father and mother were both born in Bohemia. Ancestry.com lists father as **Ernst Krumpos** (also "Krumper") and mother as **Katherine or Mary Kadlec**. If mother's surname is Kadlec, that's the same surname as Mary Katherine Kadletz who married Joseph Wenzel Stefl — the families were intermarried in the Chodsko region.
- **1880 census, Oconto County, Wisconsin** — Joseph (33, Bohemia, Farmer), Josephine (28, Bohemia, wife), Mary (9, WI), Anna (6, WI), Joseph [Samuel] (4, WI), Josephine [daughter] (2, WI), Vaclav (5 mo, WI).
