# Dorothy L. Krumpos

**Lifespan:** August 13, 1923 – March 9, 2011

## Summary
Born August 13, 1923 in Green Bay, Brown County, Wisconsin. Stay-at-home mother of six; Scouting leadership; St. John the Baptist Parish.

## Relationships
- **Parents:** Lawrence Antone Krumpos (1889–1986) & Matilda Henrietta Schneider (1897–1973)
- **Spouse:** Leo D. Matuszak (m. 1942)
- **Children:** Kenneth Matuszak, Paul Matuszak, Joseph Matuszak, Martin Matuszak, Timothy J. Matuszak, Russell Matuszak
