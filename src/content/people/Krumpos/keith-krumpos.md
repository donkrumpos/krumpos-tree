# Keith Lester Krumpos
**Lifespan:** 1950-04-18 — 2015-04-05

## Summary
Keith Lester Krumpos of Suamico passed away at home on April 5, 2015, with his wife by his side.  
Born April 18, 1950 in Green Bay, Wisconsin, to Donald and Dorothy (Coppersmith) Krumpos.  

For most of his life, Keith worked as a **project manager for the paper industry**. He was known for being neat and organized, a skilled handyman, a good cook, and enjoyed **golfing and gardening**. Most importantly, he loved taking care of and spending time with his family.
