# Betty Mae (Schmidt) Krumpos
**Lifespan:** 1947-06-28 — 1993-07-17

## Summary
Betty Mae Krumpos, 46, of New Franken, was received into "Glory" on July 17, 1993.  
Born June 28, 1947, in Green Bay, daughter of Clifford and Helen Schmidt. Married Keith L. Krumpos on April 14, 1973 in Green Bay.  

She was a very active person in many areas. Betty devoted her time and talents to her church and home. She loved crafts, sewing, cooking, gardening, and especially her family. She had a fondness for all of God’s creatures and often took in stray animals as though they were her own. She also loved her horse.  

A loving and devoted wife and mother, she truly lived for and loved her family and many friends.
