# Mae Krumpos

**Lifespan:** … – …

## Summary
Daughter of Lawrence Antone Krumpos and Matilda Henrietta (Schneider) Krumpos. Married twice: first to **Donald Albers** of De Pere (per Mathilda's 1973 obituary, which lists her as "Mrs. Donald (Mable) Albers"); later to **Dick (Richard) Willems** (per Lawrence's 1986 obituary, "Mabel and Richard Willems, De Pere"). Living in De Pere area as of 1992; still living as of 2026 — Don has lunch with her occasionally.
