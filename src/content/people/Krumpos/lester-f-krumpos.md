# Lester F. Krumpos

**Lifespan:** February 27, 1918 – November 30, 1944

## Summary
Son of Lawrence Antone Krumpos and Frances Josephine (Kosnar) Krumpos. Private, U.S. Army. Killed November 30, 1944 at Metz, France, age 26. Silver Star recipient (verify via VA). Born before mother Frances died in 1921 — one of three children from Lawrence's first marriage. Buried at Mount Calvary Cemetery, De Pere (family monument).
