# Caroline Amelia Hugunin

**Lifespan:** November 30, 1847 – September 13, 1918

## Summary
Born November 30, 1847 in Wisconsin. Daughter of David Richard Hugunin (age 30 at her birth) and Sarah Elizabeth Fuller (age 17). Married John Kendall on April 27, 1869 in Kasota, Le Sueur County, Minnesota. At least 4 sons and 4 daughters. Died September 13, 1918 in Kasota, Le Sueur County, Minnesota, age 70. Buried at Woodlawn Cemetery, Le Center, Le Sueur County, Minnesota.
