# John Loami Guernsey

**Lifespan:** December 22, 1857 – February 16, 1940

## Summary
Born December 22, 1857, Hudson, Steuben County, Indiana. Father died when he was 2. Lived in Ionia, Michigan by 1880. First wife Almeda Hinson died 1883 (Mildred was 3). Remarried Sarah E Leslie, August 19, 1891, Rapid River, Delta County, Michigan. Later lived in Louisiana (1900 census), died in Donna, Hidalgo County, Texas in 1940. Wandered far from Michigan — Mildred went to Wisconsin instead.
