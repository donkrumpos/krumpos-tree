# Mary Katherine Kadletz

**Lifespan:** c. 1822 – October 16, 1889

## Summary
Mother of Mary Katherine (Steffel) Krumpos (1845–1920). Married to Joseph Wenzel Stefl. Died October 16, 1889 in Oconto, Wisconsin. Buried at Saint Marys Cemetery, Leopolis, Shawano County, WI (same cemetery as husband).

## Relationships
- **Husband:** Joseph Wenzel Stefl (Dec 4, 1818 – c. 1895, born Klattau, Bohemia)
- **Children:** Stephen Stefl (Dec 26, 1843–1932, Klattau, Bohemia), Mary Katherine Steffel (Apr 16, 1845–Mar 26, 1920)

## Naming
Recorded variously as **Mary Katherine Kadletz** (legal/genealogical) and **Katie / Kate Stefl** (daily and on her gravestone). Common pattern for Bohemian-immigrant women of her generation: a formal "Mary Katherine" on records, with "Katie" used in life and at death. Surname variants: Kadletz, Kadlec.

## Gravestone
Confirmed marker at Saint Marys Cemetery, Leopolis. Photo taken August 10, 2002 (date stamp visible in image). Inscription, partially weathered:

```
KATIE
WIFE OF
[J.] STEFL
BORN 1822
DIED
OCT 16 1889
```
