# Pierre Joseph Meuron

**Lifespan:** 1789-01-28 — 1818-02-22

## Summary
Born and died in Mélin, Brabant Wallon, Belgium. Son of Philippe Jacques Meuron. Married Marie Barbe Logis (1791–ca 1858). Died at age 29, when his daughter Désirée Juliana was only 2 years old. Marie Barbe raised the family as a widow.
