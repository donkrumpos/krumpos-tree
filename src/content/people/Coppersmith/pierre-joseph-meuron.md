# Pierre Joseph Meuron

**Lifespan:** 1789-01-28 — 1818-02-22

## Summary
Born and died in Mélin, Brabant Wallon, Belgium. Son of Philippe Jacques Meuron. Married **Anne Marie Barbe Logis** (1791–ca 1858). Died at age 29 on February 22, 1818 — leaving Désirée Juliana (age 2) and Hortence (newborn or about to be born; possibly born posthumously). Anne Marie Barbe raised both daughters alone in Mélin for the next ~38 years.

## Relationships
- **Spouse:** Anne Marie Barbe Logis (1791–ca 1858; possible Wisconsin emigrant 1856 — see her stub)
- **Children:**

  - Hortence Meuron (1818–1880) — possibly born posthumously after Pierre's Feb 22, 1818 death
