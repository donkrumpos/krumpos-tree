# Robert Coppersmith

**Lifespan:** 1936 — 1947-03-02

## Summary
Born in Green Bay to Claude J and Marie Ellen (Bodoh) Coppersmith. Died March 2, 1947 in Green Bay at age 11. Buried at Allouez Catholic Cemetery and Chapel Mausoleum in Green Bay.

Robert died as a child. Cause of death unknown. He is listed among the deceased siblings in both Roger's (2018) and Florence's (2020) obituaries.

## Research Notes
- **Cause of death unknown** — for an 11-year-old in March 1947 Green Bay, candidates include polio (Wisconsin had outbreaks late-1940s), accident, pneumonia/post-flu complications, or scarlet fever. The *Green Bay Press-Gazette* death notice from early March 1947 would name cause.
- Died same year Dorothy married Donald Krumpos (Sept 27, 1947). Dorothy was 19, Robert had just died 6 months earlier — Dorothy's wedding sat against the family's most recent grief.
- Buried at Allouez Catholic Cemetery alongside parents Claude (d. 1987) and Marie (d. 1998). Robert was the first of Claude + Marie's family to be buried there — established the family plot 40 years before his parents joined him.
- Spacing of children before/after Robert: Florence 1932 → **Robert 1936** → Ronald ~1939. Three-year gaps either side suggest no infant losses immediately adjacent; the 1929→1932 gap (between Leonard and Florence) is the more likely site of an unrecorded loss.
