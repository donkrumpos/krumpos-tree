# Stephanie Coppersmith

**Lifespan:** 1850 — 1922

## Summary
Seventh known child of Alexis Joseph Copesmette and Désirée Juliana Meuron. Born 1850 in Mélin (probable). Came to Wisconsin with the family in March 1856 at age ~6. Lived past her mother's 1891 death (died 1922 at age ~72) — **likely one of the "three surviving daughters"** in Désirée's 1891 obituary.
