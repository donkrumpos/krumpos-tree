# Isidore Joseph Coppesmette

**Lifespan:** 1842-11-22 — 1911

## Summary
Born in Mélin, Brabant, Belgium to Alexis Joseph Copesmette and Julienne Désirée Meuron. Emigrated to Wisconsin. Married Celestine Lancelle (b. 1853, Beauvechain, Belgium) on July 9, 1871 in Robinsonville, Brown County, Wisconsin. Mélin and Beauvechain are neighboring villages in Walloon Brabant — the families likely knew each other in Belgium.

At least 5 sons and 4 daughters. Lived in Red River, Kewaunee County, Wisconsin for about 25 years. Died 1911 in Green Bay at age 69. Buried at Allouez Catholic Cemetery and Chapel Mausoleum, Green Bay — same cemetery where grandson Claude and wife Marie would later be buried.

**Name evolution:** Alexis spelled it **Copesmette** (one 'p'), Isidore used **Coppesmette** (two 'p's), Americanized to **Coppersmith** by the next generation.
