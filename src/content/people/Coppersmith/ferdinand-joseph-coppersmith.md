# Ferdinand Joseph Coppersmith

**Lifespan:** 1852 — ?

## Summary
Eighth known child of Alexis Joseph Copesmette and Désirée Juliana Meuron — and **the second of two named Ferdinand**. Born 1852 in Mélin (probable). Came to Wisconsin with the family in March 1856 at age ~4.

**Two-Ferdinand naming pattern:** he was given the same first name as his eldest brother **Ferdinand Désiré** (b. 1835, still alive at this child's birth). Walloon Catholic naming custom occasionally allowed two siblings to share a first name when distinguished by middle/calling name (the elder went by "Désiré," the younger by "Joseph"). Worth confirming via Mélin baptism register.

**Likely predeceased his mother:** Désirée's 1891 obituary lists 2 surviving sons (Désiré and Isidore); this second Ferdinand isn't one of them, so he likely died between 1852 and 1891. No death year recorded in Geneanet.
