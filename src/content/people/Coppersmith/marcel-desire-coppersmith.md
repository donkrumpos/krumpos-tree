# Marcel Désiré Coppersmith

**Lifespan:** 1838 — 1891

## Summary
Second known child of Alexis Joseph Copesmette and Désirée Juliana Meuron. Born 1838 in Mélin (probable). Came to Wisconsin with the family in March 1856 at age ~18. His name **reuses his older brother Désiré's name** as middle name — Walloon naming custom; can indicate the older brother was thought lost (he wasn't) or simply a name-share between siblings.

**Notable:** died 1891, the same year as his mother Désirée (d. July 18, 1891). Whether he died before or after her — and whether he was one of the "6 predeceased children" in the obit or alive at her death — is unresolved. Worth checking Wisconsin vital records and Walloon-area cemetery rolls for an 1891 Marcel Coppersmith death.
