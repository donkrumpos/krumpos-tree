# Alexis Joseph Copesmette

**Lifespan:** 1811-03-06 — 1889-07-26

## Summary
Born in Mélin, Brabant, Belgium to Pierre Joseph Copersmith and Marie Francoise Vanham. Married Julienne Désirée Meuron (also spelled Julianne). They had **11 children**, including Isidore Joseph Coppesmette (1842–1911).

Emigrated from Belgium to Wisconsin. Died July 26, 1889 in Red River, Kewaunee County, Wisconsin — the same community where his son Isidore lived for 25 years.

**Name evolution:** Pierre Joseph used **Copersmith**, Alexis used **Copesmette**, Isidore used **Coppesmette**, Americanized to **Coppersmith** by Frank's generation.
