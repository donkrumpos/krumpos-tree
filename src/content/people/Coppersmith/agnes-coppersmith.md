# Agnes Antoinette (Coppersmith) Larkin

**Lifespan:** 1922-04-18 — 2005-02-12

## Summary
Born in Green Bay to Frank Joseph Coppersmith and Odile Clemens Barette. Claude Coppersmith's sister — 15 years younger. Married Danford William Larkin Jr on October 28, 1939 in Sturgeon Bay, Door County, Wisconsin. She was 17. At least 1 son and 1 daughter.

Lived in Sturgeon Bay (1940), then Townsend, Oconto County (1950). Died February 12, 2005 in Green Bay at age 82. Buried at Nicolet Memorial Gardens, Green Bay.

From the memoir: "Great-Aunt Aggie" would sit with Marie Coppersmith and Dorothy Krumpos crocheting during *Dallas* and the evening soaps. Part of the Friday/Saturday night ritual at Grandma Dorothy's house on Nicolet Drive.
