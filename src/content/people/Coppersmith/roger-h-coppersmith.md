# Roger H. Coppersmith, Sr.

**Lifespan:** 1943-07-04 — 2018-07-08

## Summary
Born in Green Bay on the Fourth of July. Served in the US Army. Worked 40+ years at Georgia Pacific. Enjoyed golfing with his daughter, shooting pool, traveling, and spending time at the farm with his grand and great-grandchildren.

Children's mother: Carol Hunsucker. Four grandchildren, seven great-grandchildren. Grandson Roger Coppersmith III preceded him in death.
