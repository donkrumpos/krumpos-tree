# Antonette Potier

**Lifespan:** 1859 — 1914

## Summary
Mother of Odile Clemens (Barrette) Coppersmith (1883–1972). Also listed as Antoinette Marie Potier on some records. Married Joseph Barrette (1855–1933). Died 1914 — Odile was 31, and Odile's eldest son Wilfred was 8.

Ten children per Geneanet (1880–1897). Died 1914 when youngest was about 17.
