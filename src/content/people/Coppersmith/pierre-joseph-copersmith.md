# Pierre Joseph Copersmith

**Lifespan:** 1760 — 1828 *(Ancestry-sourced; pending cross-verification)*

## Summary
Father of Alexis Joseph Copesmette (1811–1889) and **at least 6 other children** (per MyHeritage). Married Marie Francoise Vanham (1777–1851). From Mélin, Brabant Wallon, Belgium. Earliest known Coppersmith / Copesmette / Copersmith ancestor.

Per Ancestry, born 1760 — would have been **~51 at Alexis's birth in 1811**, and **17 years older than his wife Marie** (b. 1777). The age gap is unusual enough to flag as a research thread (possible second marriage for Pierre, possible Ancestry error). Died 1828, when Alexis was 17 — about 7 years before Alexis's marriage to Désirée Meuron and 28 years before the family emigrated to Wisconsin. The family was rooted in Mélin for at least two generations before emigrating.
