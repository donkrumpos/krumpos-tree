# Elvira Coppersmith

**Lifespan:** 1847 — 1886

## Summary
Sixth known child of Alexis Joseph Copesmette and Désirée Juliana Meuron. Born 1847 in Mélin (probable). Came to Wisconsin with the family in March 1856 at age ~9. **Predeceased her mother by 5 years** (died 1886 at age ~39) — therefore one of the "6 children predeceased her" in Désirée's 1891 obituary.
