# Odile Clemens (Barette) Coppersmith

**Lifespan:** 1883-04-05 — 1972-12-07

## Summary
Born April 5, 1883 in Dyckesville, Brown County, Wisconsin to Joseph (Ferdinand Joseph) Barrette (1855–1933) and Antonette (Antoinette) Potier (1859–1914). Married Frank Joseph Coppersmith on June 6, 1905 in Red River, Kewaunee County. Mother of **five children**: Wilfred (William), Claude, Harold, Geraldine, Agnes.

Lived in Green Bay from her marriage in 1905 onward. Died December 7, 1972 (Thursday) at a Green Bay hospital after a short illness, age 89. Last address: **136 Garfield St., Green Bay**. Visitation at **Malcore Funeral Home**, Funeral Mass at **SS Peter and Paul Catholic Church** (Rev. Earl Schuh, Monday 11 a.m.). Buried at Allouez Catholic Cemetery and Chapel Mausoleum (Section F South) beside Frank.

Outlived her husband Frank by 13 years (d. Oct 7, 1959). At her death she had **23 grandchildren and 36 great-grandchildren**.

FamilySearch lists her as "Odile Clemens Barette" — Clemens appears to be a middle name, Barette the maiden surname.
