# Joseph Barrette

**Lifespan:** 1855 — 1933

## Summary
Father of Odile Clemens (Barrette) Coppersmith (1883–1972). Also listed as Ferdinand Joseph Barrette on some records. Married Antonette Potier (1859–1914).

Ten children per Geneanet: Clemence Josephine (1880), August (1882), Odile C. (1883), Oliver J. (1885), Jules J. (1887), Clemence (1889 — name reused, first likely died young), Gustave William (1890), Louis Joseph (1893), Emma (1894), Joseph (1897).

## Research Notes
- Barrette is a French/Belgian surname — consistent with Dyckesville/Door County Belgian corridor.
- Union, Door County (where Clemence was born) and Dyckesville (where Odile was born) are both in the Belgian settlement area.
- Exact birth/death dates and burial unknown — may be behind MyHeritage paywall or on FindAGrave.
- 1900/1910 census should show the household.
- **Open lead:** May be connected to Clement Joseph Barrette (1808–1892, Beauvechain, Belgium → Kewaunee County). Clement's son Julien Joseph Barrette (1833, Beauvechain) married Marie Victoire Delisse and had 14 children — but Julien married in 1860, and Joseph was born 1855, so Joseph is likely NOT Julien's son. Joseph may be Julien's brother (another son of Clement), from either Clement's first wife Eleonore Josephe Jopplet or second wife Ann DeVillers (1829–1916). Clement's known children list (Dennis, Theodore, Adolph, Desirah — all 1861–1875) doesn't include a Joseph, but may be incomplete. Unconfirmed — needs census records or parish registers to link.
