# Désirée Juliana Meuron

**Lifespan:** 1815-12-02 — 1891-07-18

## Summary
Born in Mélin, Brabant Wallon, Belgium to Pierre Joseph Meuron (1789–1818) and Marie Barbe Logis (1791–ca 1858). Same village as her husband Alexis Joseph Copesmette (1811–1889). Her father died when she was 2 years old. They had 11 children, including Isidore Joseph Coppesmette (1842–1911).

Emigrated from Belgium to Wisconsin with Alexis. Died July 18, 1891 in Green Bay, two years after Alexis. Both lived into their mid-70s in Wisconsin after ~54 years of marriage.

Appears under multiple name variants across records: Julienne Désirée, Julianne Desiree, Désirée Juliana; surname as Meuron or Meurens.
