# Hortence Meuron

**Lifespan:** 1818 — ca 1880

## Summary
Younger sister of **Désirée Juliana Meuron** (1815–1891) — the only known sibling of our direct-line ancestor. Born 1818 in Mélin to Pierre Joseph Meuron (1789–1818) and Anne Marie Barbe Logis (1791–ca 1858). **May have been born posthumously** — Pierre died February 22, 1818; if Hortence was born after that date, she never met her father. Raised by their widowed mother alongside Désirée.

Marriage and descendants unknown. Whether she emigrated to Wisconsin with Désirée's family + their widowed mother in March 1856 (age 38) or stayed in Belgium is unresolved. Died around 1880, eleven years before Désirée.
