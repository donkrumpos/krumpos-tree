# Leonard C Coppersmith

**Lifespan:** 1929-12-22 — 2003-10-21

## Summary
Born in Green Bay to Claude J and Marie Ellen (Bodoh) Coppersmith. Married Virginia Karen Pokorny on August 28, 1958 in Green Bay. At least 2 sons. Died October 21, 2003 in Green Bay at age 73. Buried in De Pere, Brown County, Wisconsin.

Second child of Claude and Marie (after Dorothy, born April 1928). Died about 18 months after his sister Dorothy (May 2002).
