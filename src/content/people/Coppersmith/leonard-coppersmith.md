# Leonard C Coppersmith

**Lifespan:** 1929-12-22 — 2003-10-21

## Summary
Born in Green Bay to Claude J and Marie Ellen (Bodoh) Coppersmith, second child after Dorothy (April 1928). Married **Virginia Karen Pokorny** on August 28, 1958 in Green Bay — Leonard 28, Virginia 18 (her birthdate was July 14, 1940). They had **six children** spanning ~1959–1970s. The family was in **Chicago** by January 1960 when their son Rickie was born there; eventually returned to Green Bay.

Died October 21, 2003 in Green Bay at age 73 — about 18 months after his sister Dorothy (May 2002). Buried in De Pere, Brown County, Wisconsin (likely Mount Calvary, same cemetery as Dorothy). Virginia survived him by 5+ years (d. 2009-02-04, retired from St. Norbert College, buried Lawrence Cemetery).

## Cross-line: Pokorny double-marriage
Leonard's wife Virginia was the sister of **Carol Hunsucker**, who was the children's mother for Leonard's brother **Roger H. Coppersmith**. Two Coppersmith brothers married two Pokorny sisters. Confirmed via Virginia's 2009 obit listing Carol Hunsucker among her siblings (also: George Jr, Ralph, Paul, John, Terri, Elaine Harn, Kathy Anderson, Connie Davis, David [deceased]). George and Josephine Pokorny had at least 11 children — a large Czech/Bohemian family in Green Bay.
