# Mabel Reinke Obituary (1992)

Mabel Reinke, 83, 2997 St. Anthony Dr., Green Bay died Wednesday morning, April 1, 1992 at a local nursing home following a long illness. The former Mabel Ruth Schneider was born Dec. 23, 1908 in Kenosha and was the daughter of the late Mathilda and Von Wilhem Schneider. She married Harold Reinke in Cedar Rapids, IA in 1947. Mabel moved from Cedar Rapids to Green Bay in 1957. She was a member of Grace Lutheran Church in Green Bay.

Survivors include one sister-in-law, Mrs. Paul (Ida) Schneider; nieces and nephews, Dorothy and Leo Matuszak, Dorothy Krumpos, Rose Mary and Donald Koening, Lloyd and Carol Krumpos, all of Green Bay; Lawrence and Shirley Krumpos, Mae and Dick Willems, De Pere; Delores Auburn and Lorraine Leavitte, both of Canada; 11 great-nieces, 25 great-nephews.

Friends may call at Ryan Funeral Home, 305 N. 10th St., De Pere, after 5 p.m. Friday until time of service. Funeral service 8 p.m. Friday at the funeral home with the Rev. Warren Egebo of Grace Lutheran Church officiating. Burial in Mt. Calvary Cemetery.

The family wishes to thank the caring staff at Guardian Angel Nursing Home.
